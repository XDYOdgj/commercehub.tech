Super Size Ironing Board Cover

Enhance your ironing routine with this durable, non-slip cover that fits securely on your board. Designed for maximum comfort and efficiency, it's perfect for both casual and professional use.

Key Features

  • Non-slip elasticized edges and nose pocket for secure fit
  • High temperature resistant material for durability
  • Easy to install directly on your ironing board
  • Breathable surface for efficient steam transmission
  • Strong bungee elastic and three hook and loop straps for staying in place

Why Choose This Cover?

This supersize cover (54x15 inch) ensures even pressure distribution, helping you achieve a wrinkle-free finish on every garment. It's ideal for delicate fabrics as well as heavy-duty items, thanks to its heat-resistant fabric that protects the board from damage. The breathable design also allows steam to escape quickly, preventing dampness and improving the overall ironing experience. Whether you're a busy parent or a fashion enthusiast, this cover adds convenience and quality to your daily laundry tasks.
