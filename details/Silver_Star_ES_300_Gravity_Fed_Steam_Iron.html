Silver Star ES-300 Gravity-Fed Steam Iron

Efficient ironing has never been easier. The Silver Star ES-300 features a gravity-fed steam system that delivers continuous moisture without the hassle of a boiler. Simply suspend the water bottle above your ironing surface to keep your fabrics smooth, wrinkle-free, and perfectly pressed every time.

Key Features

  • Gravity-fed steam system for continuous moisture
  • Non-stick laminate sole plate for smooth ironing
  • Built-in sheathed wire heater for quick heating
  • Includes demineralizer cartridge for cleaner steam
  • Silicone iron rest for safe and secure use

Why Choose the Silver Star ES-300?

This iron is designed to make your ironing routine more efficient and less frustrating. Whether you're managing a busy household or working in a professional setting, the Silver Star ES-300 is built for durability and consistent performance. Its sleek, sturdy build ensures long-lasting use, while the thick steam output ensures your clothes are wrinkle-free in no time. The included demineralizer cartridge helps keep your iron in top shape by preventing mineral buildup, reducing maintenance, and extending the lifespan of your appliance.
